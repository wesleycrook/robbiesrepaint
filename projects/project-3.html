Project 3

We recently helped a family make a newly purchased house feel more like their home. With most of the interior to do, we started with the room that least matched their personal taste. With a low-luster paint selected we needed to be sure to do an extra thorough job removing any imperfections in the drywall. Matte paint does a good job of hiding imperfections while the low luster tends to show them more clearly. The project is ongoing, so we'll be posting more photos (including some exterior shots) when we begin work on the outside.
