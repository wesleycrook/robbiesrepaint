Which Quality of Paint Should I use?

We use Sherwin-Williams paint, and there is a vast variety. Depending on the type of customer will determine the kind of paint you need. If you are a commercial customer with hundreds of rental properties, you will need to keep your bill low. Using cost-effective commercial paint such as "Pro-mar 700" is a good option for reaching those goals.

Residential customers, you will need a mid-high grade paint to save you in the long run. A high-quality paint will last for many years and hold its color. I recommend a product line called "Cashmere" it looks marvelous and goes on smooth.
