Our story

Our story begins in Augusta Georgia, during the summer of 1980 when my father Wayne started painting homes. What he found was a passion for learning this challenging craft. My father then passed his years of experience to me, his oldest son. Today I am excited about sharing his affection for painting and serving our customers.

Our services

Walls

Painting walls can be challenging so, let us do it for you. Our professional painting techniques will have your walls looking brand new.

Trim

A sloppy trim job looks awful. We know how to paint straight lines for beautiful looking baseboards, windows, and casings.

Doors

First impressions are important! We restore faded and chipping doors. The sun is harsh so, let us keep your exterior doors looking fresh.

Patching

We repair all types of damaged drywall such as nail holes, doorknob holes, or even entire sheets of drywall. Our repairs are smooth and ready to paint!

Color Matching

Love your current colors? Then let us perform a color match and freshen up your walls. Painting the same color costs less!

Prep Work

Excellent prepping will result in a great looking finish. We sand, caulk, and prime everything before we paint anything.

Stairs

Stairs can be a dangerous place to maneuver. Often we use specialty ladders to paint these areas safely. Don't risk falling; call us!

Hard-to-reach Areas

Are you the DIY type, but can't quite reach those high spots? We will gladly bring whatever equipment it takes to get the job done.
